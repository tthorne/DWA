About The Team

Now you are probably wondering about the members of the design team, well there really isn't a team there is only one person, and I am Tara.

I began my design career as a graphic artist at my grandparent’s printing company where I began teaching myself how to use the Adobe Creative Suite programs such as Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign. I eventually decided to give web design a shot, and began teaching herself HTML, CSS, some PHP, and MySql, and made it a hobby of mine designing websites for friends. After my grandparent’s printing company was sold, I found myself bouncing from one job to another when I finally decided to take web design from a hobby to a career, but I felt that I need some professional help with learning some of the other languages such as Javascript, and PHP. I had learned about Full Sail University from a friend that was attending, and after taking time to think about it I decided it was time to return school, and entered their Web Design and Development Bachelors online program. I’m still currently studying, but with graduation very near on the horizon.
